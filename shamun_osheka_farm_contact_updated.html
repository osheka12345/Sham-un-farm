Welcome to Our Farm

We are a local agricultural business located in Giza, Keana LGA. Our goal is to promote food security and sustainable farming practices.

About Us

Sham’un Osheka Integrated Farm is a fast-growing agricultural venture founded with a vision to contribute to Nigeria’s food security while empowering local communities through hands-on agricultural skills and knowledge.

Founded by Sham’un Osheka, a passionate biochemist and educator, the farm focuses on ethical and productive farming methods that benefit both people and the environment.

  • Sustainability
  • Community Empowerment
  • Knowledge Sharing
  • Integrity

Our Products & Services

Poultry Farming

We rear healthy chickens and turkeys, focusing on both broilers and layers. Our poultry products are raised with care and without harmful chemicals.

Crop Production

We cultivate seasonal crops such as maize, guinea corn, yam, and vegetables using improved farming techniques suited to the local climate.

Agricultural Training

We offer short courses and mentorship for youths and local farmers on topics like organic farming, composting, poultry management, and agribusiness startup strategies.

Farm Consultancy

We provide guidance for starting your own farm—from planning to execution.

Sales of Farm Products

Eggs, fresh produce, chicks, manure, and more—available in Giza and surrounding areas.
